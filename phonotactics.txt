>> stress
no stress distinction within a word
stress on the penultimate syllable of syntagms (clauses)
stress on the last syllable of interrogative syntagms

>> gemination
nasals geminate, no guidelines or restrictions on gemination
"long" vowels /e u ɑ ə o/ are realized [x:] at the end of the word

*note: [ɑ], [i] become [a], [ɪ] before coda [d]
